About

I'm an Industrial Engineering student at Texas Tech University and a Richmond local, driven by a simple idea: most operations have hidden inefficiencies, and the right data reveals them.

As an Industrial Engineering Intern at International Energy Services Limited (IESL), I shadowed senior engineers, took part in safety audits and facility walkthroughs, mapped workflow processes, and identified operational bottlenecks. My background in customer service and operations at Staybridge Suites® taught me how to keep things running smoothly in a fast-paced, people-first environment.

I care about continuous improvement, safety, integrity, and cost-effectiveness — and I'm looking for internship / co-op opportunities for Summer 2027, with a strong interest in operations and data center / infrastructure projects.
